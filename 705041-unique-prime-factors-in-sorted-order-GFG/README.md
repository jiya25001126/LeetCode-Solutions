# [Unique Prime Factors in Sorted Order](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/prime-factors5052/1)
## Easy
Given a number n.&nbsp;Find its unique prime factors in increasing order.
Examples :
Input: n = 100
Output: [2, 5]
Explanation: Unique prime factors of 100 are 2 and 5.

Input: n = 60
Output: [2, 3, 5]
Explanation: Prime factors of 60 are 2, 2, 3, 5. Unique prime factors are 2, 3 and 5.

Constraints:1 ≤ n ≤ 106
