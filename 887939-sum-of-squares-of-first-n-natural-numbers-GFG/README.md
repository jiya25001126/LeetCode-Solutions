# [Sum of Squares of First n Natural Numbers](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/sum-of-squares-of-first-n-natural-numbers/1)
## Easy
Given an integer n. The task is to calculate the sum of the squares of the first &nbsp;n natural numbers.
Examples:
Input:&nbsp;n = 2Output:&nbsp;5Explanation: 12 + 22 = 5
Input:&nbsp;n = 3Output: 14Explanation: 12 + 22 + 32 = 14
Constraints:0 &lt;= n &lt;= 103
