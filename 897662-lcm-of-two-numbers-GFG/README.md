# [LCM of two numbers](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/lcm-of-two-numbers/1)
## Easy
You are given two positive integers a and b&nbsp;Your task is to compute and return the Least Common Multiple (LCM) of the two numbers.The LCM of two integers is the smallest positive integer that is divisible by both a and b.
Examples:
Input: a = 12, b = 18Output: 36Explanation: LCM of 12 and 18 is 36
Input: a = 5, b = 11Output: 55Explanation: LCM of 5 and 11 is 55
Constraints:1 ≤ a, b ≤ 104
